Step 1: Meet Our Team

We get to know you and your business. We want to understand what makes you unique in your market and what your goals are. We want to know:

  • What locations do you specialize in.
  • Are you looking to grow a team or work independently.
  • What are your super agent powers.

Step 2: We Pass On Only Quality Leads

We source online leads from a number of locations like Zillow, Facebook, Truila, AdWords, Realtor.com, Move.com & more. Through a combination of phone calls, personalize email and qualification questions we determine if a lead is worth your time. If they are we schedule a showing or appointement with you.

Our qualification process includes finding out things like:

  • Have they been pre-approved for a loan.
  • Have they've already been out looking at homes.
  • Are working with another agent.
  • Are they are just out kicking the tires.

Step 3: You Go To Work

Now it's your turn to shine. Spend your time doing the things you got into this business for:

  • Find the perfect home for a buyer.
  • Nail the staging for a seller and see them smile.
  • Negotiate the best price for a client.
  • Sheppard a first time buyer through Escrow.

Step 4: Grow Your Relationship After the Sale

We beleive that friends make the best clients, so we work to turn your past clients into friends.

Through a series of well timed and well thought out events we help you engage clients like friends.

During this stage for each past client we will:

  • Comment on thier interesting facebook posts, like vacations, holidays, birthdays and parties.
  • Congradulate new promotions or jobs on linkedIn.
  • Twice yearly send gifts, like champaign on the anniversary of a clients home purchase. Or house cleaners for a spring cleaning.
  • Three times a year we'll pay for you to go to lunch or dinner with them to catch up.
