What we do

During the onboarding process, our agent’s current practices will be reviewed and according to the results, some of these services may be increased in scope, decreased in scope, or substituted for other items that appear to be a more effective use of all parties’ time. Our final service package will be custom designed with a specific focus on the items that will offer the highest return given each agent’s particular circumstances.

Custom heat maps generated with

  • Predictive analysis of agent’s farms in order to highlight homes more or less likely to sell.
  • Average home values broke down on a block by block area.

New preloaded iPad delivered directly to our partner agents

  • Containing custom selected apps to give partner agents an information edge in the field.
  • Mapped to our IT department so that any technical issues can be quickly rectified.

Custom Branding Assistance

  • A unique style guide will be generated to be used in all future marketing material.
  • Existing marketing material will be reworked to fit with the updated branding style. Including:
    • Our agent’s mailers
    • Business cards
    • Listing packet material
    • Social media accounts

Complete website redesign including

  • A homepage designed to match with the agent’s updated branding!
  • Updated page for visitors initially considering buying and selling.
  • An about the agent page.
  • A crawlable sitemap allowing for local SEO ranking.
  • Ongoing site hosting on a secure file server.

An ongoing gifting process designed to maximize referrals

  • A specific gifting system designed to increase referrals from existing clients will be unrolled. This system will feature three tiers:
    • Basic tier: All past clients receive several postcards per year in relation to events including:
      • The holidays
      • Birthdays
      • The anniversary of their close date
      • Another date selected by their agent
    • Tier 2 (10 past clients) includes:
      • All gifts under Tier 1
      • Champagne shipped on the anniversary of their close date along with a card wishing their house a “happy birthday”
    • Tier 3 (5 past clients) includes:
      • All gifts under Tier 2
      • A spring cleaning for each of your elected high value clients
      • A client specific holiday gift each year
        • This will be dependant on our shared information in terms of the client’s interests or pets.

New Listing Marketing Assistance

  • New listing pages linked to the agent’s homesite will be generated featuring:
    • A style consistent with the agent’s new branding strategy.
    • Professional photography.
    • 3D walk throughs (when applicable).
    • Retargeting code that will allow the agent to serve display ads to site visitors elsewhere on the internet.
      • A number of display ads to serve with each listing when the agent elects to employ these retargeting capabilities will also be generated.
    • An integration with our agent’s calendars allowing site visitors to book their showings directly from the site
      • This booking process will also incorporate a few quick lead scoring questions, ensuring that our agents will not walk into any showings without adequate information on the individuals they will be meeting with.
  • New listings will be uploaded to the MLS and relevant systems.
    • Agents’ social media sites will be updated with each listing.
    • Post sale: new buyers will be congratulated on the agent’s social media accounts (an action that should generate a favorable initial interaction with the client’s network.

Inbounds will be quickly responded to and follow up calls will be scheduled

  • A way for clients to easily book their showings directly from the agent’s site will be provided.
  • 24/7 answering service will be provided which will:
    • Relay important calls.
    • Forward along all messages.

Text alerts when agents’ flagged houses have an update will be dispatched

  • Texts will be sent directly to agents when houses they’ve flagged have an update.

A system for partner agents’ clients’ to track which houses they’ve viewed will be provided

  • Before our agents show a house we will preload an editable document available to both the agent and the client that will include:
    • The home’s address.
    • A few pictures of the home.
    • A summary of the listing.
    • The home’s current status.
    • A series of questions designed to standardize the comparison between different homes that clients viewed.
  • The system will be regularly updated with additional information on the visited houses’ availabilities.

ESCROW Assistance

  • Transaction Management.
    • All of the agent’s documentation will be onboarded to our virtual system.
    • Time sensitive documentation will automatically be introduced in a timely manner and followed up upon.
  • An inspection will automatically be scheduled as soon as ESCROW starts (for buyers).
    • An email will be sent the day before the inspection containing:
      • The common structural and aesthetic issues encountered during the inspection process.
      • Outline our contingency plan for each of them.
  • An appraisal will be automatically scheduled as soon as ESCROW starts (for buyers).
  • Client stress throughout the ESCROW process will be managed in a number of ways by facilitating open communication and transparency.

Weekly Process Summary e-mails with indication as to what to expect in the coming week will be authored

  • Emails will be assembled for the agent that:
    • Summarize the actions taken last week.
    • Highlight what clients may need to prepare for for next week.
    • Will be sent to the agent for any edits and final approval.
      • Once the agent is satisfied he or she can pass along the email to their client, taking complete credit for it.

Listing Package and Appointment Assistance

  • Agent’s listing packets will be updated with their new branding style.
  • Custom marketing contingency plans (a detailed, scheduled out marketing plan for if a house were to remain on the market for any extended period of time) built out for each meeting.
  • Agents will be provided with personality profiles of each of the individuals they are sitting down to meet with before the meetings
    • These profiles will also contain information about how active the agent’s prospects have been in relation to the agent’s marketing materials.

Partner agents will be submitted annually for award consideration

  • Local and national awards that fit the profile of partner agents will be identified.
  • The submission for partner agents will be completed once a year.
  • Any received rewards will be announced on social media and added to the agent’s bio.
